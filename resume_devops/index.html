Analyzing and Streamlining LMS Automation in Azure

The business was facing challenges with a malfunctioning script for extracting tables from email and sending them to the vendor via FTP for their Learning Management System (LMS). The script was causing delays and errors in the data transfer process and impacting the overall efficiency and effectiveness of the LMS. I performed a root cause analysis using Pandas and PowerBI dashboard, queried 4 years worth of log records, and identified patterns and anomalies in the data that were indicative of the failure. Based on the findings, I provided a brand new cloud-native solution for business continuity, built using modern cloud technologies such as microservice and serverless to ensure scalability, high availability, and resilience. The implementation of this solution resulted in improved efficiency and effectiveness of the LMS, reduced delays and errors in the data transfer process, provided valuable insights into the data transfer process, and prevented similar failures from occurring in the future.
